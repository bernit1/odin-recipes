Accidental Fish

Description

This accidental fish recipe is one of my family's favorite fish recipes for Southern-style cooking. Serve this dish over rice. Steamed vegetables go great with it.

Ingredients

  • 2 (4 ounce) fillets mahi mahi
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • Â½ cup salted butter
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 drops Louisiana-style hot sauce, or to taste
  • 1 Roma tomato, seeded and chopped (Optional)
  • 1 green onion, chopped

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
  2. Rub fillets with oil; transfer to a baking dish.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until fish flakes easily with a fork, about 20 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Add lemon juice, garlic, and hot sauce; simmer for 1 minute. Add tomato and green onion; cook and stir until heated through. Spoon over fillets to serve.
